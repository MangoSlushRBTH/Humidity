Chart showing how mold growth, viral transmission, and asthma risk each vary across the humidity spectrum, with a recommended healthy zone of 35–50% RH where all three risks are minimized.

Mold / fungi growth Viral transmission Asthma / allergic rhinitis
Mold risk is low below 60% RH and rises sharply above. Viral transmission is highest below 30% and above 70%. Asthma risk is elevated at both extremes with a trough at 40–50%. The optimal zone is 35–50% RH.
Curves are schematic representations based on Sterling (1985), Lowen et al. (2007), Shaman & Kohn (2009), and ASHRAE Standard 55. Relative risk is normalised to 0–100 for comparability across categories. The recommended zone (35–50% RH) reflects the range where all three risk curves simultaneously reach or approach their minima.
